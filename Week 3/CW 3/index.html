Fun Facts About Milk Frogs

1. Amazon milk frogs are found in the tropical rainforests of South America.

2.These amphibians received the name "milk frog" from the sticky white substance they secrete through their skin when threatened. This substance is poisonous to potential predators.

3. Milk frogs are arboreal, and spend their lives in trees and other plants. Milk frogs have special toe-pads on their feet to help them climb plants. They can hold up to 14 times the animal's body weight!
